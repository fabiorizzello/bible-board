## Locked decisions from context

- `src/ui/mockups/UnifiedEditorMockup.tsx` is the primary canonical reference for `02-03`.
- Previous sketch artifacts remain rationale only; they are not competing sources if they diverge.
- `02-03` must cover inline per-field UX, non-tabular detail layout, coherent edit primitives, drawer-based complex editing, Milkdown for descrizione, unified add-field flow, and linked review/validation affordances.
- S03 scope stays out: do not absorb full fonti/catalog editing into this plan.
- Dev-only mockup routes remain available until the end of M002.

## Constraints

- Preserve the existing domain contract rather than re-opening it: `ElementoInput`, `normalizeElementoInput`, `ElementoTipo`, and `ElementoError` stay the source of truth for validation.
- Do not mutate `src/mock/data.ts`. Visible edits should be modeled as session overrides layered on top of the immutable mock dataset.
- HeroUI usage must follow the documented compositional APIs already adopted in this repo:
  - `TextField` owns `value`, `onChange`, and validation; `Input`/`TextArea` are child slots.
  - `Dropdown`, `Popover`, and `Drawer` use dot-notation anatomy.
  - `Toolbar` should provide the action cluster container instead of bespoke keyboard-less wrappers.
- Fullscreen and non-fullscreen detail must share the same editing behavior.
